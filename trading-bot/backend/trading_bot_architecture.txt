TRADING BOT: TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE & OPERATIONS GUIDE
=======================================================

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
-------------------
The bot is an automated trading system for MetaTrader 5 (MT5), specializing in Smart Money Concepts (SMC). It identifies institutional footprints like liquidity sweeps, order blocks, and market structure shifts. It currently runs on a specialized "BotWorker" engine in Python.

2. FULL TRADE LIFECYCLE
-----------------------
- STARTUP: Connects to MT5, validates Demo-Only status.
- SYNC: Fetches M1, M5, M15 candle data.
- SCAN: Evaluates 8 SMC strategies against closed candles.
- AI CHECK: Filters trades based on a 0.48 confidence threshold.
- RISK CHECK: Guards on spread (max 55), stale ticks (<10s), and late entry (<70% move).
- ORDER: Sends order using IOC/RETURN/FOK retry logic.
- MANAGEMENT: 
  * Stage 1: Close 50% at 60% progress, move SL to Break-even. 
  * Stage 2: Close 25% at 80% progress, advance SL.
- SYNC: Identifies broker-side exits and records final exit reason (TP2, SL, etc.).

3. ENTRY LOGIC (STRATEGIES)
---------------------------
- Sweep Reclaim: Detects sweeps of M15 High/Low on M1.
- VSA Shift: Volume spikes at Supply/Demand zones.
- Continuation: Trend continuation via M5 Order Block mitigation.
- Mitigation: Touching M15 FVG boundaries.
- MSS Shift: Market Structure Shift on M1 timeframe.
- Breaker Block: Retest of a failed Order Block.
- Exhaustion: Oversold/Overbought reversal via RSI + CHoCH.
- Volume Flow: Price interaction with Volume Profile POC.

4. STOP LOSS AND TAKE PROFIT
----------------------------
- SL: Structural (Swing H/L) + buffer. Fallback to 1.5x ATR.
- TP: Two targets calculated (TP1/TP2). TP2 is the hard MT5 target.
- MONOTONIC GUARD: SL can only move in favor of protection (never backwards).
- PROGRESS: Calculated as (Price - Entry) / (TP1 - Entry).

5. PARTIAL CLOSE MATH
---------------------
- VOLUMES: Stage 1 = 0.50x, Stage 2 = 0.25x (of initial).
- CLOSED_FULL: Triggered if remaining volume would be < 0.01 lot step.
- RECOVERY: Bot halts new trades if an SL update fails after a partial, retrying until safe.

6. RISK FILTERS
---------------
- SPREAD: Max 55 points (override for Gold).
- RE-ENTRY: Skips symbol scan if an open trade is detected.
- GAP LOCK: Prevents toxic risk if protection updates fail.

7. CONFIRMATION STATUS
----------------------
- SL Guard: Confirmed (Strict implementation).
- Volume Math: Confirmed (Handles XM lot steps).
- Filling Modes: Confirmed (IOC retry logic).
- AI Score: Logic integrated, needs long-term demo validation.

8. KNOWN RISKS
--------------
- news spikes can cause false "Absorption" signals.
- No cooldown exists immediately after an SL hit if the setup reforms.
- High-impact news can hit the spread filter and block trades.
